ENGLISH:
--------
desc1: Hello, my name is Ricardo Linhares. I am a psychologist and a full member of the Portuguese Order of Psychologists (no. 27338).
desc2: I hold a degree in Psychology, a master's in Work and Organizational Psychology, and an advanced specialization in Clinical and Health Psychology.
desc3: I began my career in the Organizational field, where I conducted numerous recruitment and selection interviews, professional profile analyses, and studied the impact of psychosocial risks (such as burnout) on people's personal and professional lives.
desc4: Later, I became interested in exploring other areas of psychology. I then founded 'Ricardo Linhares Psychology Practice' as a way to work in psychological health in general and to contribute to research and the publication of knowledge about psychology.
desc5: In my Psychology Practice, I focus my work on what are called emotional disorders — that is, states in which emotions are disturbed, such as depression, burnout, stress, anxiety, obsessive-compulsive patterns and panic attacks.
desc6: As a method of psychological intervention, I use two of the most current and important psychological models in recent years: Acceptance and Commitment Therapy and the Unified Protocol (the unification of different variants of Cognitive-Behavioural Therapy), with extensive specific training in these approaches.
